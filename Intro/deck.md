## The Assumption

If something becomes more efficient, we'll use less.

Faster. More optimized. Less consumption. Does it?

---

## Jevons Paradox

Efficiency increases total consumption.

Note: In 1865, Jevons observed that improved coal engine efficiency led to more coal being consumed, not less.

----

## 1865

Coal engines became more efficient.

----

## What Happened?

Coal consumption increased.
