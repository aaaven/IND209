# Design Portfolio

## Behavior-Consequence Mapping

### Description

A portfolio page presenting 28 individual works. Beginning with ordinary AI behaviours and small acts of convenience, students traced how these interactions activate wider infrastructural processes and planetary consequences. Each work maps the relation between user action, hidden infrastructural activity, and planetary impact over time.
